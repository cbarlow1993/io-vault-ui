# Pricing Structure

## Overview

Two-module pricing structure for enterprise crypto custody and compliance platform:

- **Treasury** - Core custody module (anchor product)
- **Compliance** - Add-on module (requires Treasury)

Each module has 4 tiers: Starter, Professional, Enterprise, Custom

## Customer Scenarios

### Treasury Starter — $500/mo

**Scenario: Boutique Family Office**
A family office managing digital assets for 3 high-net-worth families. They need secure custody for Bitcoin and Ethereum holdings across 8 wallets with 4 signers (2 principals + 2 advisors). Basic approval workflow is sufficient as the principal signs off on all transactions.

- 8 wallets across 2 chains
- 4 signers, 2 admins
- Single approval workflow
- **Monthly cost: $500**

---

**Scenario: Early-Stage Crypto Fund**
A newly launched crypto hedge fund with $10M AUM. Managing 5 wallets across Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana. Small team of 3 partners who all participate in signing.

- 5 wallets across 3 chains
- 3 signers, 2 admins
- API integration with portfolio tracking
- **Monthly cost: $500**

---

### Treasury Professional — $1,000/mo

**Scenario: Regional Crypto Exchange**
A licensed exchange operating in Southeast Asia. Managing hot and cold wallets across 15 chains with separate operational wallets for trading, withdrawals, and reserves. Requires multi-level approval workflows (ops team → compliance → treasury).

- 35 wallets across 12 chains
- 20 signers across departments
- Tiered approval workflows
- Staking for proof-of-stake assets
- **Monthly cost: $1,000**

---

**Scenario: DAO Treasury**
A mid-sized DeFi protocol DAO managing protocol treasury, grants fund, and operational expenses. Multi-sig with elected council members and conditional workflows based on transaction size.

- 15 wallets across 8 chains
- 12 signers (council + contributors)
- Conditional approval logic ($10k+ requires 4-of-7)
- Webhook notifications to Discord
- **Monthly cost: $1,000**

---

### Treasury Enterprise — $2,000/mo

**Scenario: Digital Asset Bank**
A regulated digital asset bank offering custody services to institutional clients. Manages segregated wallets per client across all major chains. Requires policy engine for automated compliance checks and SSO integration with corporate identity provider.

- 180 wallets (segregated by client)
- All supported chains
- 75 signers across teams
- Policy engine enforcing transaction limits
- SSO/SAML with Okta
- DeFi yield strategies for select clients
- **Monthly cost: $2,000**

---

**Scenario: Global Crypto Exchange**
Top-50 exchange by volume operating across multiple jurisdictions. Complex treasury operations including staking, liquidity provision, and yield optimization. Requires dedicated API endpoints for high-frequency operations.

- 150+ wallets
- All chains including new listings
- 80+ signers across global offices
- DeFi integrations for treasury optimization
- Dedicated API infrastructure
- **Monthly cost: $2,000**

---
